the gist of who i am

Ever since the dawn of Adode, I've been smitten with digital design. It provided me with unlimited set of materials for creation. Adobe's Photoshop has maintained its position as the industry standard in pixel-based image manipulation and design after all this time.

This is my knife.

Illustrator has served as my gateway to vector-based design. The mere concept of mathematically rendered shapes gets me giddy. Dimensions are no longer constraints as designs can scale at will.

This is my fork.

Responsive web design echoes the same sentiments as vector graphics with its ability to adapt to any and all digital environments. One day soon...

This will be my spork.

With tools in hand, I love to carve up each new delectable dish of design goodness.
